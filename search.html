Facts About Blood Search and Usage

Blood Types

There are eight common blood types: O+, O-, A+, A-, B+, B-, AB+, and AB-. The universal donor blood type is O-, while the universal recipient blood type is AB+.

Blood Compatibility

Compatibility is important for blood transfusions. O- is compatible with all blood types, but O+ can only donate to positive blood types. Always check compatibility before a transfusion.

Blood Donation Benefits

Regular blood donation can help reduce the risk of heart disease, increase iron levels, and save lives. Donors also receive a health checkup during each donation.
